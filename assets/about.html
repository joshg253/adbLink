adbLink Overview

adbLink is a companion program for any device running Kodi.

adbLink allows easy maintenance of Kodi: edit Kodi's XML files, make backups of Kodi's data, restore those backups or clone new installations from your backups.

For Android Kodi, adbLink installs (sideloads) apps to your device without the bother of installing the Android SDK, as well as offering other Android-only functionality.

adbLink is released under the GPL V3. Source is available at http://www.jocala.com

If you have questions or comments, please email jocala@jocala.com. adbLink produces a log file, adbshield.log, with each run. Include it in your email or post if you're having problems.
