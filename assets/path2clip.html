Android only

The Path to clipboard menu function copies the command to include busybox in the Android system path. Select the menu function, then perform a "Paste" after opening ADB Shell. You will now have access to the full range of busybox applets at the Android command-line.

If you have questions or comments, please email jocala@jocala.com or visit one of the forums linked below. adbLink produces a log file, adblink.log, with each run. Include it in your email or post if you're having problems.
