View Log button adbLink keeps two logs, one log of your current run and a log of your previous run. They are named adblink.log and adblink.old.log.

Using the "View Log" menu option, you can view both these logs and copy them to your clipboard for inclusion in a support post or email.

When the Viewer opens it shows by default your current running log, adblink.log. Pressing the "Swap" toggles over to adblink.old.log. Pressing "Swap" again toggles back.

Thus you can toggle back and forth between adblink.log and adblink.log.old

If you have questions or comments, please email jocala@jocala.com or visit one of the forums linked below. adbLink produces a log file, adblink.log, with each run. Include it in your email or post if you're having problems.
